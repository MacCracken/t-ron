# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and productive environment for
everyone. All participants are expected to:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull
requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers
at conduct@agnos.dev. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
